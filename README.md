# Jesse Music Desktop

This is a browser-based audio player made with standard HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. It uses the native `<audio>` element and does not use external JavaScript libraries or audio frameworks.

## How to Run

1. Open the `Audio-Player` folder.
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser.

## What the App Does

- Plays songs from the built-in playlist
- Lets you play or pause audio
- Lets you move to the previous or next track
- Lets you skip forward or backward by 10 seconds
- Shows the current song time and total duration
- Lets you click a song in the queue to play it
- Lets you upload local audio files
- Lets you mute or adjust volume
- Lets you change playback speed
- Lets you turn shuffle on or off
- Lets you turn loop on or off
- Lets you download songs from the built-in playlist

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Space` to play or pause
- `Left Arrow` to skip backward 10 seconds
- `Right Arrow` to skip forward 10 seconds
- `M` to mute or unmute
- `S` to change playback speed
- `L` to toggle loop

## Notes

- Uploaded files are temporary and only work in the current browser session.
- Uploaded files are shown as `LOCAL UPLOAD`.
- Local uploaded files cannot be downloaded using the download button.
